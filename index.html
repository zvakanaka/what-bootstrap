Typography

Highlight text with <mark>.

Abbreviate A with <abbr title="Acronyms">A</abbr>.

Show keyboard shortcuts like ctrl + r with <kbd>ctrl + r</kbd>.

Make a definition list with dl:

Sputnik
Burned up January, 1958
Explorer
Battery died May, 1958
Stayed in orbit 12 years

<dl>
    <dt>Sputnik</dt> 
    <dd>Burned up January, 1958</dd> 
    <dt>Explorer</dt> 
    <dd>Battery died May, 1958</dd> 
    <dd>Stayed in orbit 12 years</dd>
</dl>

Tables

The .table-striped class highlights every other row:

Bike Brand Model Name Price
Specialized Rock Hopper $200
GT Outpost Trail $35
Trek 3700 $250 
<table class="table table-striped"> 
  <thead> 
    <tr><th>Bike Brand</th><th>Model Name</th><th>Price</th></tr>

Images

Thumbnails: .img-thumbnail class.

Rounded corners: .img-rounded class.

Circular: .img-circle class.

The .img-responsive class: resizes with the page width when smooshed:

<img class="img-responsive" src="dung-b.jpg" alt="Beetle" width="384" height="288">

Jumbotron

The .jumbotron class on a div or other container.
